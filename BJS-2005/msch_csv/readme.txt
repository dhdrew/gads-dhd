Multistate Criminal History Patterns of Prisoners Released in 30 States, NCJ 248942		
		
This zip archive contains tables in individual .csv spreadsheets from Multistate Criminal History Patterns of Prisoners Released in 30 States, NCJ 248942.		
The full report, including text and graphics, in pdf format is available from: http://www.bjs.gov/index.cfm?ty=pbdetail&iid=5407
